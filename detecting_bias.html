Tips on Detecting Bias in Your Everyday Life

Within the News

Check the source of the news. Is there a lack of diversity? From whose POV is the news being told from? Are there double standards? Is there a lack of context?

Within Social Media

Take into account of other people's POVs. Are certain posts being censored (certain races being cropped out of photos in preview or posts not violating community guidelines being censored)? Are posts violating guidelines (racism, sexism, nudity, etc.) not being taken down?

Within Job Applications

Are you getting jobs picked for you based on a certain stereotype (the name Jessica getting recommended "feminine" jobs for example)? Are you getting more/less jobs based on your race?

Within Recommendations

Which gender likes the topic more in your recommendation? Are you getting recommended things that are currently popular?
