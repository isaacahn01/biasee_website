Detecting Bias

“There are many statues of men slaying lions, but if only the lions were sculptors there might be quite a different set of statues.” -Aesop

Bias can be unseen from one person's perspective, but to another it can be seen in plain view. Learn how to detect bias throughout your experience on the internet and see how you can help to prevent such biases from occurring more often.

Why Bias?

Bias is a dangerous tool within machine learning and algorithms.

  • Bias may result in wrong court sentences.
  • It may result in you getting a lower credit score.
  • It may result in faulty data.
